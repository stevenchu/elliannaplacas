Launched in the Summer of 2008,  Garconiera is an effortless, stylish women’s resort wear line featuring kaftans, dresses, cover ups and separates designed to be worn from the beach to a bbq, day-to-night, city to country, around the globe.  Garconiera clothing is created as a travel-inspired, ready-to-go wardrobe that is beautiful and hand washable.

Garconiera is crystalline Australian waters promising adventure, the sensual moodiness of Greek summers, the castaway fantasies of Thailand and the inebriating vibrations of Jamaica—the island life.  The collection evokes adventure, beauty, freedom and possibility. Designs flow with colors and patterns, mirroring nature’s vivid hues and the subtle, reflective tones of sunsets and sparkling waters.

Garconiera embodies romantic rendezvous, ambrosial appetites, an afternoon siesta in a crochet hammock. Lounging on the bow of a yacht skimming jade waters, the first sip of a summer rosé. Outdoor showers, butterflies, music and motion. Friends, giggles and glances. Beauty, curiosity and spirit flow in our garments. The Garconiera woman is passionate, creative, loving and free, layered but easy, spiritedly independent. Traversing the globe to dream and create… 

Garconiera launched at Saks Fifth Avenue, Harvey Nichols, Henri Bendel and Scoop. PlanetBlue.com, 25Park.com, Tootsies and boutiques worldwide have also sold the collection.
